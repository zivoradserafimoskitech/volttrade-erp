The engine

A battery and an algorithm work for you around the clock

Behind your flat price sits real hardware and real trading. Our AI trades battery storage on the day-ahead and intraday markets — charging when solar floods the grid and power is cheap, discharging into the expensive evening peak. Every well-timed trade lowers the cost of the energy we supply you. That's how the price stays flat without games.
